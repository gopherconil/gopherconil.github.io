About Us

GopherCon is focused on
the Go programming language

GopherCon Israel 2019 is the first GopherCon to be held in Israel. The conference will be held on February in the greater Tel-Aviv area. We are expecting approximately 250 people to attend the event. GopherCon brings the Go community together through its events throughout the world. The GopherCon brand is well known and respected for its high standards and diverse appeal.
