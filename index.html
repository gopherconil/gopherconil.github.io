About Us

GopherCon is focused on
the Go programming language

GopherCon Israel 2019 is the first GopherCon to be held in Israel. The conference will be held on February 11 at the Tel Aviv Convention Center. 250 Gophers will attend the event. GopherCon brings the Go community together through its events throughout the world. The GopherCon brand is well known and respected for its high standards and diverse appeal.

Our Sponsors

GopherCon Israel relies heavily on sponsorship to run the event at rates affordable to the whole community. If you're interested in sponsoring our event, please contact us.

Speakers

There will be two kinds of talks at the conference:

  • A long talk - 40 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A
  • A short talk - 20 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A

There will be just one track in the conference, talks will be held in one room in front of all the participants.
