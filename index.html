About Us

GopherCon is focused on
the Go programming language

GopherCon Israel 2020 is the second GopherCon to be held in Israel. The conference will be held on February 3 at Expo Tel Aviv. 400 Gophers will attend the event. GopherCon brings the Go community together through its events throughout the world. The GopherCon brand is well known and respected for its high standards and diverse appeal.

Our Sponsors

GopherCon Israel relies heavily on sponsorship to run the event at rates affordable to the whole community.

Speakers

There will be two kinds of talks at the conference:

  • regular 30m talks.
  • lighting talks of 15m each.

There will be two tracks in the conference, one for beginners and another for more intermediate talks.
