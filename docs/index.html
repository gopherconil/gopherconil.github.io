About

GopherCon Israel 2026 will be a live conference.

GopherCon is focused on the Go programming language, nevertheless, the topics to be presented relate to many other domains, including: DevOps, cloud computing, web programming, hacking and more.

Call for Speakers

Open · submissions close July 31

We're looking for a diverse range of sessions for both newcomers and seasoned Gophers. This year we're focused on building AI systems — but there's plenty more we'd love to hear about. Sessions should bring practical, real-world takeaways the audience can apply.

Topics we love

  • AI systems
  • Concurrency
  • Web development
  • Microservices
  • Tooling
  • Performance
  • Security
  • Community projects
